Tips about Search Engine Optimazation

On page SEO tips

  • The URL link should shows a clear hierarchy of the information and the intended purposes of the page.
  • The layout of the page should be unique.
  • The content of the page should be designed to closely relates to the page's intended purpose.
Reference: On-Page SEO Ranking Factors

Crawler Bot Premissions

  • By allowing certain Crawler Bot to access your site and to index your content so others can get to your page by simply searching.
  • by disallowing crawlers to access certain pages, you could prevent a good crawler from spidering your potentially private and important informations
  • malicious crawlers often ignores Robot.txt.
Reference: What is a Robots.txt
